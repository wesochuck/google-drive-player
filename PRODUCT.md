# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Music enthusiasts and casual listeners who want a clean, simple, and distraction-free way to play their MP3 playlists. They value a minimalistic aesthetic and a focused audio experience.

## Product Purpose
A lightweight, web-based MP3 playlist player that prioritizes speed and simplicity. It provides a modern interface for navigating folders and playing audio tracks without the bloat of traditional media players.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Elegant, Rhythmic. The brand (Chorus) should feel like a well-tuned instrument—functional yet beautiful, with a focus on high-quality details and smooth interactions.

## Anti-references
- Clunky native file explorers
- Ad-heavy web players
- Overly complex DJ software or "professional" workstations
- Saturated SaaS "cards everywhere" dashboards

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Clutter**: Only show what is necessary for the current task (playing or browsing).
- **Rhythmic Interaction**: Every state change should feel smooth and intentional, mirroring the flow of music.
- **Modern Minimal**: Use high-quality typography and strategic color to create hierarchy, not borders or boxes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Full keyboard navigation for playback controls.
- Clear focus states for all interactive elements.
- WCAG AA contrast compliance for all text.
